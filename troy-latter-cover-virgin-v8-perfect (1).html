Re: AI Transformation Lead

I've spent 15 years making complex technology work in regulated, operationally-intensive environments — and making sure people actually adopt it. That second part is where most AI programs fail.

Here's what most airlines miss: the journey doesn't start at the airport. It starts at the decision to travel. And it doesn't end at baggage claim — it ends when the memory is made or the meeting is won. AI can transform every moment in between: personalised offers at the right time, frictionless identity, rebooking before you know there's a problem, loyalty that actually knows you. Virgin's brand is already loved. The opportunity is making every touchpoint feel as good as the cabin crew.

My thesis: Technology without change management is expensive shelfware. Every AI initiative I lead includes adoption metrics, human partners, feedback loops, and a change plan. I'm Prosci ADKAR certified — not as a credential, but because I've seen too many brilliant systems die from neglect.

At AWS, I led Australia's post-COVID border reopening strategy — coordinating airlines, airports, Australian Border Force, and health agencies to physically reopen international travel. Travel corridor pilots to New Zealand. Digital health credentials. Ministerial briefings. Aviation transformation under extreme uncertainty, with no margin for error.

At Oracle and Unisys, I delivered emerging technology programs at Changi Airport, Hong Kong International, and across Australia — passenger systems, cargo, security screening, innovation labs. At Defence, I managed change and communications for technology rollouts across 100,000 staff globally.

At Tech 4 Humanity, I've built Holo-Org — a 5,000-agent AI platform where every agent sits alongside a human function with defined scope, handoffs, and adoption metrics. It's my answer to the question: how do you make AI actually stick?

Virgin's tech stack is ready: SabreMosaic on Google Cloud, Databricks, Confluent Kafka powering the Flight State Engine, an OpenAI partnership. What's needed now is someone to turn that infrastructure into guest delight — fewer calls to the contact centre, faster rebookings, loyalty that feels personal, and sales that feel helpful rather than pushy.

I'm certified across Virgin's stack: AWS Solutions Architect, Azure AI Engineer, GCP Architect. I work daily with OpenAI, Claude, Databricks, Snowflake, and agentic frameworks (LangChain, LangGraph, MCP).
